!battle(["Aurora",:DREAMCATCHER,0], "Darkness Field")

This is a challenging battle, on a completely new field. Not dissimilarly from Reborn's Flower Garden field, it has stages that make Dark and Ghost types progressively stronger. You can reverse the stages via moves like Dazzling Gleam, Wish, and Flash Cannon, or by using Sunny Day or Drought if you want to weaken Aurora, though she has a few moves like Ominous Wind that can advance the stages. Also an option is leaning into it: Sleep moves are made more potent on this field, as are your own Dark and Ghost moves. Your own Magical Seeds can help, too.

As far as her team goes, it's a lot of Ghost and Dark types. A lot of pivoting in and out of good matchups should make the bulk of her team easy to handle. Her Mega Mightyena is the biggest threat, gaining a Speed boost upon Mega Evolving that makes it quite powerful and speedy. It's still not the bulkiest thing in the world, so two or three Pokemon with survivability against its boosted moves can probably take it out.

## Route 4 Town

No longer besieged, the Route 4 Town area is now explorable. 

!battle(["Larry",:FISHERMAN,0], "Grassy Terrain")

In a house towards the middle of the town, you'll get to start the **Missing Parcel** sidequest. One more quick trip up all the way to the top of Hardened Mountain will reward us with the *Parcel* to return in exchange for *4 Credits* and a *Flame Orb*.

!battle(["Jeff",:FISHERMAN,0], "Grassy Terrain")

Having accepted **Infiltration** from Lucas in the Rangers Guild earlier, you can talk to Myrtle on one of the piers to the Uncharted Island area. Grab the hidden *Heart Scale* and proceed. Talk to the NPC in the middle of the dining room above when you're ready for another gauntlet.

!battle(["Kate",:BLACKFOXF,0], "Grassy Terrain")

!battle(["Pluto",:BLACKFOXM,0], "Grassy Terrain")

Back on Route 4, continue south from the pier. Grab a hidden *Heart Scale* and proceed west.
